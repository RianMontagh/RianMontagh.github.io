# Summer Goals and Conveyance Analysis

For the summer quarter, I want to zoom out to the wider project goals to help me formulate a plan for my summer research. 

## Overall Investigation Goals
1. Will a large flood or combinations of floods cause an avulsion in the Nooksack
2. What other parameters affect the 'avulsivity' of the system during a flood?

## Specific Research Goals to Address Overall Ivestigation Goals
1. What are ways that I can quantify the "avulsivity" of the system?
2. How can I force an avulsion to happen? Is it even possible under our current model conditions?
3. How can I understand what is causing changes in bed, discharge, etc?

## Ways to Address my Specific Research Goals
1. Remove the forested bar that may be preventing an avulsion channel from incising into the floodplain.
2. Investigate the cause of Shleby's avulsion.
3. Finish quantifying conveyance changes in the main channel.
4. Run several floods in a row instead of just two. Maybe avulsions need constant repeated flooding in order to form.
